Banana Pancakes

Banana pancakes are a delicious breakfast treat that combines the sweetness of ripe bananas with the comforting warmth of pancakes.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Start by mashing the ripe bananas in a bowl until they are smooth. You can use a fork or a potato masher for this.

step 2

In a separate mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

step 3

In another bowl, beat the egg and then add the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract (if using). Mix well.

step 4

Combine the wet ingredients with the mashed bananas and stir until they are well mixed. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.

step 5

Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium-high heat and add a small amount of oil or butter to grease the surface. Pour a portion of the pancake batter onto the hot skillet to form pancakes of your desired size. Cook until you see bubbles forming on the surface and the edges start to look set, usually 2-3 minutes.

step 6

Flip the pancakes and cook for another 1-2 minutes on the other side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Remove the banana pancakes from the skillet and keep them warm while you cook the remaining batter.

step 7

Serve your banana pancakes with toppings of your choice, such as maple syrup, sliced bananas, chopped nuts, or whipped cream.

ingredients

1 1/2 cups dry pancake mix

1/2 teaspoon baking soda

1 cup milk

1 egg

1 2 tablespoons melted butter

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
