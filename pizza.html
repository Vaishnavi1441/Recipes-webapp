Pizza

Pizza is a popular and beloved Italian dish that has become a global culinary sensation. It typically consists of a round, flat dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of ingredients like vegetables, meats, and seasonings.

prep time

45 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Snacks

instructions

step 1

Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can go, typically around 475-500°F (245-260°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven as it preheats.

step 2

Sprinkle some flour or cornmeal on a clean, flat surface to prevent the dough from sticking.

step 3

Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape. You can make it round, square, or any shape you like. If you prefer a thinner crust, roll it out more; for a thicker crust, keep it thicker.

step 4

If using a pizza pan or baking sheet, grease it lightly with olive oil or cooking spray. If using a pizza stone, dust a pizza peel or another flat surface with cornmeal or flour to prevent sticking.

step 5

Carefully transfer the rolled-out dough to your prepared pizza pan, baking sheet, or pizza peel if using a stone. Spread a layer of pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.

step 6

Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese (or your preferred cheese) over the sauce. Add your favorite toppings, such as pepperoni, vegetables, olives, or any other ingredients you like.

step 7

If using a pizza stone, carefully slide the pizza onto the hot stone in the preheated oven. If using a pan or baking sheet, simply place it in the oven. Bake for about 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown, and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. Allow the pizza to cool for a few minutes before slicing it into pieces using a pizza cutter or a sharp knife.
